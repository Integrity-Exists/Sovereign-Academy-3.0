🔥 Remove a Biased or Unethical GAL

If your Guardian ad Litem is doing more harm than good, you have legal tools to fight back. They are *not above the law* — and we’ll show you how to hold them accountable.

🔍

    🚩 Reasons to Remove a GAL

    • Clear conflict of interest (represented another party previously)
    • Refusing to interview both parents
    • Withholding evidence
    • Making false statements or showing obvious bias
    • Failing to visit the child or provide neutral reporting

    📄 What to File

    1. Motion to Remove Guardian ad Litem – cite specific reasons and include evidence
    2. Affidavit – your sworn statement showing why the GAL must be removed
    3. Request a hearing – demand to be heard before further damage is done
