What to Do If DSS Shows Up

  1. Stay calm and do not allow entry without a warrant.
  2. Ask for identification and request their business card.
  3. Do not answer questions without legal representation.
  4. Document everything: who came, what time, what was said.
  5. Politely refuse entry unless they present a valid court order or there’s a clear emergency.

⚖️ Know Your Rights

  • 📜 Right to remain silent: You do NOT have to answer questions or sign anything without a lawyer.
  • 🏠 Right to privacy: DSS cannot enter your home without a court order or emergency.
  • 👩‍⚖️ Right to legal counsel: You can request a public defender or legal aid.
  • 📸 Right to record: In Virginia, you may legally record your own conversations — document everything.
  • 🛑 You can say NO: Refuse drug tests, interviews, or inspections unless ordered by a judge.
